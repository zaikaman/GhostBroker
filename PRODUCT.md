# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Institutional trading operators, SREs, and compliance managers who observe and audit automated dark-pool trading executions. They require real-time system telemetry and verifiable proof of enclave execution without exposing active hidden order books.

## Product Purpose
To provide a secure, attested, and compliant dashboard (observatory mode) for monitoring institutional AI agents executing trades within hardware-secured enclaves.

## Brand Personality
Attested, Confidential, Uncompromising

## Anti-references
Neon crypto-slop charts, complex multi-color grids, corporate SaaS-blue templates, and noisy decorative widgets.

## Design Principles
1. **Zero Visibility Integrity**: Visual design must highlight the cryptographic barrier shielding active orders (humans may only observe system state, not trading data).
2. **Utilitarian Elegance**: Information hierarchy prioritized over decoration; spacing is clean, metrics are clear, and colors are functional.
3. **Hardware Attested Confidence**: Subtle green accents indicate attested/secure enclaves, evoking security and trust.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
High-contrast text under variable lighting conditions (dark/dim rooms), keyboard navigable components, and clear color state indications supported by icons/text labels.
